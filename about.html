Our Story

We're just a group of students passionate about books and learning. This is our class project turned into a fun little bookshop!

Why We Started

For our web development class project, we wanted to build something useful and interesting. Since we all love reading finance and self-improvement books, we decided to create WealthWise Books.

It's not a real store (yet!), but we had fun designing it and learning along the way.

Our Mission

To make financial education accessible and enjoyable through carefully selected books. Our aim is to spark curiosity, promote informed decision-making, and encourage a lifelong interest in personal finance.
